# From SEO to Strategy: A Decade of Digital Excellence

- December 29, 2023

Since 2016, Steven Al-hakim has dedicated his career to mastering the art and science of digital marketing. With nearly 10 years of solid experience in the field, Steven’s journey has been defined by continuous growth, adaptability, and a deep passion for helping brands stand out in an increasingly competitive digital world.
Steven began his path in marketing as an SEO Specialist, diving into the complexities of search engines and how brands can organically rise to the top. From there, he expanded into media buying, social media strategy, and content creation—building a well-rounded digital skill set that allowed him to manage campaigns from every angle.
His capabilities quickly earned him senior roles, eventually becoming Digital Marketing Manager for a company operating in the Saudi stock market—a highly competitive and regulated industry, where performance, trust, and compliance are everything.
But Steven’s vision extended beyond corporate roles. In 2022, he established his own digital marketing agency: Veiltail.

### Veiltail – Built to Make Brands Irresistible
Founded with the core belief that every brand has the potential to be a “unique piece” in its market, Veiltail quickly positioned itself as a boutique-style marketing powerhouse. In just three years, the agency helped grow over 25 brands, each with its own story, challenges, and unique identity.
Steven led marketing campaigns across a wide range of industries, including:
- Home Furniture & Interior Design
- Finishing & Decoration
- Dentistry & Dental Clinics
- Physiotherapy, Weight Loss & Wellness
- Restaurants & Food Concepts
- Jewelry, Accessories & Stainless Fashion
- Creative Industries: Photography, Makeup, and more
This variety of experience became one of Steven’s greatest assets—allowing him to understand different customer behaviors, market dynamics, and content needs.

### The Trainer Who Trains to Transform
Steven isn’t just a strategist—he’s also a teacher at heart. Over the years, he has trained more than 1,000 aspiring marketers and business owners across Egypt, Kuwait, and Sudan through live workshops and collaborations with both local institutions and international partners.
His training sessions are known for being practical, engaging, and insight-driven, often blending technical skills with real-world business thinking. From solo freelancers to corporate teams, Steven’s goal is to empower others to market with confidence, clarity, and creativity.

### A Consultant with a Philosophy
What sets Steven apart is not just his knowledge—but his philosophy. As a marketing consultant, his mission is simple:
> To help every client become a brand that’s impossible to ignore.Not just a product or a service—but a story, a feeling, a unique piece that lives in people’s minds.
This vision is embodied in Veiltail’s motto:“Be a unique piece that feeds on shrimp.”—A bold metaphor that reflects the agency’s belief in positioning, excellence, and standing out in your niche.
