How We Tripled Furnist's Online Leads in 90 Days

The Challenge

Furnist was struggling to generate online leads and faced stiff competition. Their online presence was not converting visitors into customers, and their marketing efforts lacked a clear strategy.

The Solution

We developed a comprehensive digital strategy that included a website redesign, a targeted social media campaign, and the creation of high-quality media content. We focused on showcasing their unique furniture designs and building a strong brand identity.

The Results

Within 90 days, Furnist saw a 350% increase in online sales, a 200% increase in qualified leads, and a 500% growth in social media engagement. The new website and creative campaigns successfully captured the brand's essence and resonated with the target audience.
